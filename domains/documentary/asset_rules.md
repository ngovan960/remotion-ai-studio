# Documentary Asset Rules

## Image Assets

### Required Types
- **Photographs**: High-resolution historical or contemporary images
- **Documents**: Letters, maps, newspapers, official records
- **Artifacts**: Physical objects, tools, clothing
- **Landscapes**: Location establishing shots
- **Portraits**: People relevant to the story

### Image Quality Standards
- Minimum resolution: 1920x1080
- Color profile: sRGB
- Format: PNG or high-quality JPEG
- No watermarks or logos
- Consistent color grading across scenes

### Image Sources
- Unsplash (free license)
- Pexels (free license)
- Local archive libraries
- Generated illustrations (when real images unavailable)
- AI-generated photorealistic images (with disclosure)

## Video Assets

### Stock Footage Guidelines
- License: Royalty-free or Creative Commons
- Resolution: 4K preferred, 1080p minimum
- Frame rate: 24-30 fps
- Style: Cinematic, slow-motion acceptable
- Subject: Relevant to documentary topic

### Generated Video
- Use Remotion for animated sequences
- Photo montages with Ken Burns effect
- Text animations for facts and statistics
- Map animations for geographical context

## Text Assets

### Typography
- Font: Clean, readable sans-serif (Helvetica, Arial)
- Size: Minimum 24pt for subtitles, 36pt for titles
- Color: White with drop shadow for contrast
- Animation: Fade in/out, no flashy effects

### Text Content Rules
- Fact-check all text before including
- Use consistent date formats
- Include source citations when appropriate
- Avoid subjective language in text overlays

## Audio Assets

### Music Selection
- Genre: Ambient, orchestral, piano, acoustic
- Mood: Contemplative, hopeful, serious
- Tempo: 60-80 BPM for narration scenes
- Key: Minor keys for serious topics, major for hopeful

### Sound Effects
- Nature: Wind, water, birds, insects
- Urban: Traffic, crowds, machinery
- Period: Historical sounds when available
- Transitions: Subtle whooshes, risers

## Asset Organization

### Directory Structure
```
assets/
├── images/
│   ├── portraits/
│   ├── locations/
│   ├── artifacts/
│   └── documents/
├── video/
│   ├── stock/
│   └── generated/
├── audio/
│   ├── music/
│   └── sfx/
└── text/
    ├── titles/
    └── overlays/
```

### Naming Convention
```
[domain]_[type]_[subject]_[variant].[ext]
Example: documentary_image_portrait_historian_01.jpg
```
