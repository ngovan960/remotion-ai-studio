# Documentary Quality Rules

## Quality Philosophy
Documentary quality is measured by credibility, clarity, and engagement. Every element must serve the story and maintain professional standards while preserving authenticity.

## Visual Quality Standards

### Image Requirements
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1920x1080
- **Color Profile**: sRGB for web, Adobe RGB for print
- **Format**: PNG for graphics, JPEG for photographs
- **Compression**: Minimal quality loss
- **Consistency**: Uniform style throughout

### Video Quality
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 minimum
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps standard, 24 fps cinematic
- **Bitrate**: 8-12 Mbps for 1080p
- **Codec**: H.264 or H.265
- **Color Space**: Rec. 709

### Text Quality
- **Readability**: Clear at all sizes
- **Contrast**: High against backgrounds
- **Animation**: Smooth, not distracting
- **Consistency**: Same style throughout
- **Accuracy**: Fact-checked content

## Audio Quality Standards

### Voiceover Quality
- **Clarity**: Every word intelligible
- **Consistency**: Same volume throughout
- **Noise**: No background hum or hiss
- **Pacing**: Natural, not rushed
- **Emotion**: Appropriate to content

### Music Quality
- **Selection**: Matches mood and tone
- **Volume**: Balanced with voiceover
- **Transitions**: Smooth between tracks
- **Licensing**: Proper rights secured
- **Relevance**: Supports story

### Sound Effects
- **Quality**: High-fidelity recordings
- **Timing**: Synced with visuals
- **Volume**: Appropriate levels
- **Relevance**: Enhances, not distracts
- **Consistency**: Same quality throughout

## Narrative Quality

### Story Structure
- **Clear Beginning**: Establish context
- **Development**: Build understanding
- **Climax**: Emotional or informational peak
- **Resolution**: Satisfying conclusion
- **Cohesion**: All parts connect

### Information Delivery
- **Accuracy**: Fact-checked content
- **Clarity**: Easy to understand
- **Pacing**: Appropriate speed
- **Balance**: Not too much, not too little
- **Relevance**: All content serves story

### Emotional Impact
- **Authenticity**: Genuine, not manipulative
- **Connection**: Relatable to audience
- **Engagement**: Maintains interest
- **Resonance**: Lasting impression
- **Appropriateness**: Matches content gravity

## Technical Quality

### Performance Metrics
- **Render Time**: Within acceptable limits
- **File Size**: Optimized for distribution
- **Compatibility**: Works on target platforms
- **Accessibility**: Meets standards
- **Reliability**: Consistent output

### Error Prevention
- **Testing**: All elements verified
- **Backup**: Project files preserved
- **Version Control**: Changes tracked
- **Documentation**: Process recorded
- **Contingency**: Plans for issues

- [ ] Risk assessment completed

### Production
- [ ] Each scene meets standards
- [ ] Audio levels balanced
- [ ] Transitions smooth
- [ ] Text readable
- [ ] Pacing appropriate

### Post-Production
- [ ] Color grading consistent
- [ ] Audio mixed properly
- [ ] Effects subtle and relevant
- [ ] Final review completed
- [ ] Export settings verified

### Distribution
- [ ] Multiple formats created
- [ ] Metadata complete
- [ ] Quality checks passed
- [ ] Backup copies made
