# Horror Story Asset Rules

## Image Assets

### Required Types
- **Dark Photography**: Low-light, atmospheric shots
- **Horror Art**: Dark illustrations and paintings
- **Generated Horror**: AI-created dark imagery
- **Stock Dark**: Horror-themed stock photos
- **AI Generated**: Custom horror imagery

### Image Quality Standards
- Minimum resolution: 1920x1080
- Color profile: Dark, desaturated
- Format: PNG for graphics, JPEG for photos
- No watermarks or logos
- Consistent dark aesthetic

### Image Sources
- Horror photography collections
- Dark art communities
- AI image generators (with horror prompts)
- Custom dark photography
- Stock sites with horror categories

## Video Assets

### Horror Scenes
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum
- Frame rate: 24 fps (cinematic)
- Lighting: Low-key, dramatic
- Movement: Deliberate, unsettling
- Effects: Minimal, purposeful

### Generated Video
- Dark ambient animations
- Jump scare sequences
- Photo montages with horror effects
- Glitch and static effects
- Shadow and light play

## Atmosphere Assets

### Dark Settings
- **Abandoned Places**: Hospitals, asylums, factories
- **Isolated Locations**: Forests, mountains, rural areas
- **Confined Spaces**: Basements, attics, closets
- **Night Scenes**: Urban, rural, interior
- **Decaying Environments**: Ruins, derelict buildings

### Lighting Elements
- **Candles**: Flickering, warm but ominous
- **Flashlights**: Moving beams, limited visibility
- **Moonlight**: Cold, blue, casting shadows
- **Flickering Lights**: Unstable, threatening
- **Darkness**: Absolute, oppressive

## Horror Elements

### Entity Representation
- **Silhouettes**: Dark shapes, suggestion only
- **Shadows**: Moving, independent of objects
- **Glimpses**: Quick, partial reveals
- **Distortions**: Visual glitches, reality warping
- **Absence**: Something missing that should be there

### Environmental Horror
- **Decay**: Peeling paint, rust, rot
- **Wrongness**: Things out of place
- **Movement**: Things that shouldn't move
- **Reflections**: Showing something different
- **Time**: Clocks stopped, wrong time

## Audio Assets

### Voiceover
- **Quality**: Intimate, close-mic'd
- **Tone**: Whispered, urgent, fearful
- **Pace**: Slow, deliberate
- **Effects**: Reverb, echo, distortion
- **Emotion**: Genuine terror

### Music Selection
- **Genre**: Dark ambient, horror soundscapes
- **Mood**: Dread, tension, fear
- **Tempo**: Slow, building
- **Key**: Dissonant, minor
- **Volume**: Dynamic, building

### Sound Effects
- **Ambient**: Creaking, wind, dripping
- **Whispers**: Unintelligible, directionless
- **Heartbeat**: Building, accelerating
- **Impacts**: Sudden, loud
- **Silence**: Absolute, oppressive

## Text Assets

### Typography
- **Font**: Distorted, handwritten, or clean contrast
- **Size**: Variable, sometimes hidden
- **Color**: Pale, red, or invisible against dark
- **Animation**: Glitching, fading, appearing
- **Style**: Minimal, atmospheric

### Text Content Rules
- **Language**: Simple, direct
- **Tone**: Urgent, warning
- **Placement**: Unexpected locations
- **Timing**: Brief, impactful
- **Effect**: Subliminal, unsettling

│   ├── music/

### Naming Convention
```
[domain]_[type]_[element]_[variant].[ext]
Example: horror_image_hallway_abandoned_01.jpg
```
