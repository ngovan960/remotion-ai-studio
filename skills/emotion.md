# Emotional Design Skill

> Thiết kế cảm xúc cho video

## Emotion → Visual Mapping

| Emotion | Lighting | Camera | Color | Movement | Music |
|---------|----------|--------|-------|----------|-------|
| **Fear** | Dark, low key | Close-up, handheld | Desaturated, cold | Slow, shaky | Dissonant |
| **Joy** | Bright, warm | Medium, steady | Saturated, warm | Energetic | Major key |
| **Sadness** | Dim, soft | Close-up, static | Muted, blue | Slow, still | Minor key |
| **Anger** | Harsh, high contrast | Low angle, Dutch | Red, dark | Fast, aggressive | Intense |
| **Surprise** | Sudden change | Zoom, snap | High contrast | Quick movement | Stinger |
| **Mystery** | Shadows, partial | Slow reveal | Dark, desaturated | Slow, deliberate | Ambient |
| **Hope** | Sunrise, golden | Wide, upward | Warm, bright | Gentle movement | Building |
| **Tension** | Flickering, unstable | Handheld, shaky | High contrast | Erratic | Suspense |

## Emotional Arc for 10-min Video

```
Time:  0    2    4    6    8    10
       │    │    │    │    │    │
Hook:  ▲▲▲▲                                    (Curiosity)
Setup:     ▲▲▲▲                                 (Interest)
Conflict:      ▲▲▲▲▲▲                           (Tension)
Midpoint:            ▲▲▲▲▲                      (Surprise)
Escalation:              ▲▲▲▲▲▲▲              (Urgency)
Climax:                        ▲▲▲▲▲▲▲▲▲      (Peak)
Resolution:                           ▲▲▲▲    (Relief)
```

## Emotion in Prompts

```
Emotion: [fear/joy/sadness/anger/surprise/mystery/hope/tension]
Lighting: [emotion-specific lighting]
Camera: [emotion-specific angle]
Movement: [emotion-specific speed]
```

Examples:
- `Fear: dark room, close-up, handheld, desaturated, slow movement`
- `Joy: bright golden hour, medium shot, warm colors, energetic movement`
- `Mystery: shadows, slow reveal, dark palette, deliberate movement`
