# Cinematography Skill

> Kĩ thuật quay phim cho AI video production

## Shot Types & Meanings

| Shot | Khoảng cách | Ý nghĩa | Khi nào dùng |
|------|-------------|---------|---------------|
| **Extreme Wide** | Rất xa | Establishing, scale | Opening scene |
| **Wide Shot** | Xa | Context, environment | Scene setting |
| **Full Shot** | Toàn thân | Character introduction | Meet character |
| **Medium Wide** | Từ đầu gối lên | Action + context | Movement scenes |
| **Medium Shot** | Từ eo lên | Conversation, interaction | Dialogue |
| **Medium Close-up** | Từ ngực lên | Emotion + action | Key moments |
| **Close-up** | Mặt | Strong emotion | Climax, reaction |
| **Extreme Close-up** | Chi tiết | Emphasis, detail | Important objects |

## Camera Angles & Psychological Effect

| Angle | psychological Effect | Use |
|-------|---------------------|-----|
| **Eye Level** | Neutral, equal | Normal conversation |
| **Low Angle** | Power, dominance | Hero, authority |
| **High Angle** | Weakness, vulnerability | Victim, defeat |
| **Dutch Angle** | Unease, tension | Horror, thriller |
| **Bird's Eye** | Scale, overview | Establishing |
| **Worm's Eye** | Overwhelming, awe | Epic moments |

## Lens Effects

| Lens | mm | Effect | Use |
|------|-----|--------|-----|
| Wide | 16-35mm | Distortion, space | Establishing |
| Normal | 50mm | Natural perspective | Conversation |
| Telephoto | 85-135mm | Compression, isolation | Close-up |
| Macro | 100mm+ | Extreme detail | Objects |

## Depth of Field

| DoF | Aperture | Effect | Use |
|-----|----------|--------|-----|
| Deep | f/8-f/16 | Everything sharp | Landscape, establishing |
| Moderate | f/4-f/5.6 | Subject sharp, background soft | General |
| Shallow | f/1.4-f/2.8 | Subject sharp, background blur | Portrait, emotion |
| Very Shallow | f/1.2 | Only eyes sharp | Extreme emotion |

## Prompt Templates

```
Wide establishing shot, deep depth of field, [environment], [lighting]
Close-up of [subject], shallow depth of field, [emotion], [lighting]
Low angle shot of [subject], dramatic lighting, [mood]
Drone shot, aerial view, [environment], [time of day]
```
